summary: An interactive, level-tagged field guide for English learners who want
  to keep progressing outside of class. Covers routine-building, vocabulary,
  grammar, speaking & listening, pronunciation, reading & writing, and
  idioms/phrasal verbs — each item stamped Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced,
  with a filter to show only your level. Includes built-in quizzes with instant
  feedback, text-to-speech playback on key phrases/sounds, a weekly planner and
  a self-assessment progress tracker that both save automatically as the user
  works, and a curated tools & resources list. Ends with a short mindset section
  for staying motivated as a self-directed learner.
problem: "Classroom time (or a single course) usually isn't enough exposure to
  actually build fluency — real progress happens in the hours between lessons,
  but most learners don't know what to do with that time beyond \"watch a show
  in English\" or \"use an app.\" This guide replaces vague advice with a
  structured, leveled system: concrete daily routines, specific exercises per
  skill area (not just vocabulary, but pronunciation mechanics, connected
  speech, self-editing, shadowing, etc.), and built-in tools to track what's
  actually been practiced — so independent study time turns into measurable
  progress instead of passive exposure."

  - word stress

  - categorized list of external tools and resources for vocabulary

  - and reading Mindset strategies for staying motivated as a self-directed
