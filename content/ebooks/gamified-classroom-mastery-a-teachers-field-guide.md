tagline: Turning daily routines into engaging, badge-based systems.
summary: A practical field guide for turning ordinary classroom routines —
  arrival, homework, transitions, participation, collaboration — into a tiered,
  badge-based progression system. Grounded in the three drivers of intrinsic
  motivation (autonomy, competence, relatedness), the guide walks teachers
  through the anatomy of a working badge system (transparent criteria, tiered
  levels, visible progress, a real celebration ritual, and multiple paths to
  earning), maps out everyday routines worth gamifying, and provides a four-tier
  badge progression (Stardust → Moonbeam → Comet → Constellation) that gives
  students something to keep climbing toward.
problem: Many gamification attempts in classrooms either fizzle out within weeks
  or devolve into a popularity contest / prize-chasing exercise that undermines
  genuine motivation. This guide solves that by grounding badge design in
  motivational science rather than novelty, and by giving teachers concrete
  structural requirements — clear criteria, multiple tiers, visible progress
  tracking, a genuine celebration ritual, and more than one path to success — so
  a badge system stays meaningful and sustainable well past the first month of
  the term.
