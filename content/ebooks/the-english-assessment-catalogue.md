tagline: A complete collection of formative, summative, and quiz-based
  assessments for speaking, reading, and writing — designed for every learner,
  every level, every age.
summary: This is a ready-to-use catalogue of 18 asynchronous English assessments
  spanning three age groups (kids 5–11, teens 12–17, adults 18+) and four CEFR
  levels (A1–B2). Every assessment covers speaking, reading, or writing and
  includes teacher notes, a student-facing task, and a detailed scoring rubric —
  requiring no additional prep. It closes with master general rubrics for
  speaking and writing, plus a full quick-reference table of all 18 assessments
  by age, level, skill, type, time, and points.
problem: Teachers assessing English learners across different ages and levels
  often have to build assessments from scratch — matching task type to
  age-appropriate content, designing a fair rubric, and making sure the task can
  be completed independently outside class time. This catalogue solves that by
  providing a complete, plug-and-play bank of assessments that are already
  leveled, age-appropriate, rubric-scored, and designed to run asynchronously,
  so teachers can select the right test for their learners in minutes instead of
  building one from zero.
