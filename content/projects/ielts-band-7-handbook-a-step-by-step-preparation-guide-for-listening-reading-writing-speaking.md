tagline: Decoded, drilled, and mastered — the examiner's lens on breaking the
  Band 6 plateau.
summary: A 23-page, self-contained IELTS preparation handbook targeted at
  candidates already at a solid intermediate level who need to break through the
  common 6–6.5 plateau into Band 7+. It covers exam mechanics (Academic vs.
  General Training, band calculation, scoring criteria), section-by-section
  strategy for Listening, Reading, Writing (Task 1 and Task 2), and Speaking,
  each with mini timed practice tasks and answer keys, a compressed cross-skill
  mock test, an 8-week study plan, a "15 mistakes that cap candidates below Band
  7" checklist, and a final exam-day readiness checklist.
problem: Most IELTS candidates stuck at Band 6–6.5 don't lack English ability —
  they lack exam-specific strategy and the examiner's actual marking logic, so
  they keep losing marks to fixable habits (skipping the Task 1 overview,
  confusing False with Not Given, going silent in Speaking, ignoring word
  limits) rather than to gaps in language. Generic IELTS materials tend to
  either re-teach English from scratch or just supply practice tests without
  explaining why answers score the way they do. This handbook solves that by
  working backward from the marking criteria itself, isolating the specific,
  learnable behaviors that separate a 6 from a 7 in each skill, and pairing
  every strategy with a timed practice task so learners build exam stamina, not
  just knowledge.
outcome: >-
  Explain what Band 7 actually rewards across all four skills and how the
  overall band is calculated

  Apply skill-specific techniques for Listening (prediction, paraphrase recognition, distractor awareness), Reading (skim/scan/close-reading selection, question-type strategies), Writing (Task 1 overview writing, Task 2 structuring by question type, Band 7+ cohesive devices), and Speaking (the P-S-D-F framework for Part 2, extended Part 1/3 responses)

  Identify and correct the 15 most common mechanical mistakes that cap capable candidates below Band 7

  Complete a structured 8-week self-study plan building from diagnostic testing through full timed mock simulation

  Walk into the test centre with a concrete, rehearsed exam-day routine
