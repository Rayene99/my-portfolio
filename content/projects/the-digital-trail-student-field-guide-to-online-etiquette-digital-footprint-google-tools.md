tagline: An interactive map for walking your digital footprint well.
summary: 'An interactive, self-paced HTML field guide that walks students
  through two parts: digital citizenship (online etiquette, managing their
  digital footprint, safe and smart research) and a practical Google Workspace
  toolkit walkthrough (account basics, Gmail, Classroom, Docs, Slides, Gemini
  AI, and submitting assignments). It closes with an academic resources section
  and a quick-reference cheat sheet. The guide is built as a navigable "trail"
  with 12 stops, a progress tracker ("Trail Passport"), scenario-based judgment
  checks, interactive checklists, reveal-to-learn cards, and an FAQ accordion —
  making it a hands-on learning experience rather than a static handout.'
problem: Students entering an online or blended classroom are often handed
  technology without guidance — they're expected to already know how to write a
  professional email, manage what they post and share, evaluate sources
  critically, use AI tools responsibly, and navigate Google Classroom, Docs, and
  Slides. Gaps in any of these areas show up as unprofessional communication,
  poor research habits, privacy mishaps, or simple confusion over where to
  submit work. This guide solves that by consolidating digital-citizenship
  habits and platform mechanics into one self-guided, engaging resource students
  can work through independently or reference on demand — reducing repetitive
  tech-support questions for teachers and setting consistent expectations from
  day one.
outcome: >-
  Adjust tone and formality appropriately across emails, comments, and chat
  messages ("code-switching" for the right register)

  Understand and manage their digital footprint responsibly

  Apply safe, smart research practices when finding and evaluating online sources

  Set up and navigate their school Google account with confidence

  Use Gmail, Google Classroom, Docs, and Slides for academic work

  Understand appropriate, transparent use of AI tools like Gemini in schoolwork

  Correctly and confidently submit assignments through Classroom

  Apply etiquette and safety judgment in realistic scenarios via built-in scenario checks

  Reference a quick cheat sheet for fast recall of key habits and steps
